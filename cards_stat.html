CWR: Clean Win Rate. Clean Win Rate is the win rate of a card against decks that don’t have that card. Normally, CWR would be almost the same as WR, except when the card’s usage is exceptionally high, then CWR can be very different. A card is considered strong when abs(CWR - WR) > 2%. This delta value was at 10%+ for Little Prince at launch.
